Brief

A Software Engineer with proven development, communication and management skills from a Mechanical Engineering background. I am a member of Ahmadu Bello University's Software Development Team. My conquests have seen me undertake projects which span cost, efficacy and improvement areas. My main focuses are on areas such as: How well a task works , as opposed to what is already available?

Software Projects include Mobile App Development, ETL Development, Unix Scripting, Web Design and Development.

Within the four walls of the University, I am part of a team that works on web development projects such as the University's website and related paraphernalia, User online registration and development of bespoke applications using open source IDE's. I am currently cutting my teeth on Javascript and AngularJs, with a focus on Single Page Applications (SPAs) and Offline Applications using PouchDb.

Current Research

In the Digital games industry, the financial resources of gamers are usually limited. This means Digital Game Design companies have to compete for the affection of would be gaming customers.Players pick games based on certain features, which suit their gaming tastes. Limited financial resources can pose a problem for the gaming industry because potential customers become picky where competing titles exceed budgets. Some are unwilling to pay unless there is a preview hence the freemium model of games distribution. Others may take a different approach by allowing game reviews, word of mouth or other awareness campaigns to influence their game choices.

The Games Industry has committed a lot of resources to research, design and development of engaging games that would keep users committed to a franchise or title

Projects Involved In

    BMI Calculator

    The aim of the project was to create an iPhone®/iPod® Touch application that would be used for monitoring a user’s weight gain or loss. The user would store a series of weight and height measurements on the device, and be able to view his progress on a chart.

    The design phase was broken down into two phases namely:
    1. System Requirements
    2. System Architecture

    The Implementation and Testing were done using Apple's Macbook Pro.

  • Heat Treatment of Low Chromium Carbon Steel

    Testing the best form of Heat Treatment

    Low Chromium Carbon Steel is metal that is used in very abrasive conditions. It hard characteristics makes it ideal for where wear and tear due to lateral forces will be encountered. But this is the upside to its use.

    The downside is that fatigue can result in premature failure. Issues such as this makes for very difficult amortisation. Apart from the financial there are also the health and safety issues.

    After testing a few samples of LCCS through various heat treatment processes it was established that Oil Heat Treatment was the best was of salvaging toughness of steel intrinsic to longevity during usage.
